Shepherds pie

Shepherds pie is one of the most simple dinner dishes and still it is very delicious

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Green Peas
  • Beef broth
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Milk
  • Salt and pepper

Beef and vegetables

First of all you need to make sort of stew from your ground beef and vegetables. Put a dutch owen on the stowe over medium-high heat and let it heat up. Put your beef in the pot and let it caramelize , while it`s cooking chop up your vegetables. Vegetables should be cut in cubes roughly 0,5 cm thick (roughly the size of a single pea). When your beef is about 80% done add onions and carrots and let it cook for 5 minutes. Add your broth to deglaze the pan , add green peas stir well to combine. Add salt and pepper to taste and leave it simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes.

Mashed potatoes

For mashed potatoes you firstly need to peal them , then boil till soft. Heat 500 ml of milk until roughly 80 degrease celsius. Drain all the water from potatoes and start mashing it , add 3 tbsp of butter. Add 100 grams of parmesan to your potatoes and keep mashing it. Start adding milk a little bit at a time and keep stirring/mashing. When it is done it will be silky-like consistency. Add salt and pepper to taste and stir it one more time.

Baking

At this point your dish is pretty much done , you just need to layer and bake it. Put your stew on the bottom of the baking sheet and the potatoes on top. You can rough-up mashed potatoes with fork and sprinkle with parmesan to cover it. Because all of the ingredients already done , it doesn`t need too much time in owen. Put it in the owen for 15-20 minutes under the broiler , and it is done.
