Lasagna

Lasagna is a pretty easy to make , it doesn`t require lots of your attention in cooking and very delicious

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground Beef
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Red wine
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Beef broth
  • Parmesan(optional)
  • Dried basil
  • Thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Lasagna sauce

For lasagna sauce get a large pot (dutch owen works best). Put it over medium-high heat , drizzle 2 tbsp of oil and let it sit until you see a little bit of smoke. Add in your ground beef to caramelize. While your beef is cooking chop your vegetables into small cubes. When your beef is 80% done add your vegetables and mix it well. Add dried basil , thyme ,salt and pepper to taste Cook for another 5 mites , then add 1 glass of wine to deglaze. Add your tomatoes and broth , lower your heat and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes.

Beshamel

In small pot melt your butter. When it is melted add 1-2 tbsp of flour and whisk it. Cook your flour for 2-3 minutes , then add milk and stir constantly. Add salt and pepper to taste and it is done.

Assembly

Cover your baking sheet with lasagna , then add sauce , beshamel and top it off with cheeses. Repeat until the baking sheet is full , then place it in the owen for 30-40 minutes. And your delicious dinner is done!

Useful Tip

Let it cool of before cutting , so it stays in one place.
